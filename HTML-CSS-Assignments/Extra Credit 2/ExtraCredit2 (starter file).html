Three-eyed alien spotted at Harvey Mudd!

Tuesday, May 23, 2017

by R.E. Porter

Harvey Mudd Professor Zachary Dodds claims to have seen a three-eyed alien on the HMC campus late last night. "I was walking to my car after a thrilling Jotto tournament with the graders, and I saw it slip behind the Olin building and disappear from view," says Dodds. This isn't the first time an HMC professor has claimed to see the alien creature. Five sightings have been documented on the campus in the last two years. Unfortunately, there is no physical evidence to corroborate any of these claims.
